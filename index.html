Play,Learn & Grow

International Education at Affordable Cost

London Kids aims to offer high-quality global education affordably, reaching children in rural India. Our mission prioritizes accessible international education, ensuring every child, regardless of location or background, accesses superior learning opportunities, fostering a brighter future.

Who We Are

Learn About Our Work and Cultural Activities

We merge the British National Curriculum's Early Years Foundation Stage with Indian syllabus standards, focusing on practical, enjoyable learning. Our approach integrates cutting-edge technology, fostering a dynamic educational environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and holistic development, ensuring children thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape.

01

Vision & Mission

Provide UK-concept international curriculum to even the remotest parts of India at an affordable cost. Align with Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan and Beti Bachao Beti Padhao to empower children through education.

02

Educational Philosophy

Use various teaching methods (oral, written, digital, activity-based) to generate interest in learning.

03

Curriculum

Adapts British National Curriculum Early Years Foundation Stage to Indian syllabus standards. Emphasizes practical, fun learning with the latest technology.

04

Facilities

Interactive play area, modern teaching aids, Montessori equipment, and non-toxic play materials.

05

Parental Involvement

Conducts monthly parent-teacher meetings to actively involve parents in their child's early education development.

06

Safety & Security

Implements biometric attendance, CCTV surveillance, and follows the Ashoka Childcare Policy to ensure non-discrimination.

Our Classes

Play Group

Introduction to social interaction, basic routines, and play-based learning for toddlers, fostering early social and cognitive skills.

Play Group Age: 2-3 Years Time: 8-10 AM Seat: 25

Nursery

Building foundational skills through play, structured activities, and exploration, focusing on language, numbers, and basic concepts.

Nursery Age: 3-4 Years Time: 8-10 AM Seat: 25

Junior KG

Developing literacy, numeracy, and social skills through structured activities, preparing children for formal education.

Junior KG Age: 4-5 Years Time: 8-10 AM Seat: 25

Senior KG

Advancing skills in reading, writing, mathematics, and social interaction, laying the groundwork for primary schooling.

Senior KG Age: 5-6 Years Time: 8-10 AM Seat: 25
