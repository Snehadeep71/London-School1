Training videos cover Activity Calendar utilization, effective marketing strategies, and maximizing opportunities. These videos empower staff with essential skills, from curriculum planning to business strategies, fostering a well-rounded educational environment.

London Kids prioritizes comprehensive curriculum development focusing on gross motor skills, socialemotional, and cognitive growth. The founder's affiliation with the Early Childhood Association ensures quality. All franchise teachers receive a free 120-hour certified online training, integral to the franchise, enhancing their teaching expertise and enriching the learning experience.
